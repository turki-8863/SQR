Student flow

How SQR should be used.

The website works as a complete process, not just independent static pages.

1

Create account

Students sign up with secure authentication and get a profile dashboard.

2

Pick specialization

The platform shows specialization cards, details, careers, and linked courses.

3

Open courses

Opening course content auto-enrolls the student and saves progress in the database.

4

Pass quizzes

Quiz attempts complete the remaining course progress and update specialization progress.

5

Use ATS tools

Students check resume files and generate cleaner ATS-friendly resumes.

6

Apply smarter

Job recommendations and match percentages help students plan their next step.
