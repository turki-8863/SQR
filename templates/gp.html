SQR is a dynamic computer science career guidance platform connected to a real database. The system helps students explore specializations, browse courses, take quizzes, discover jobs, use ATS resume tools, and get career recommendations.

All website numbers shown here are loaded from the database, not written manually. Student progress is kept inside the profile page where it is calculated from opened courses and quiz attempts.
